## 📌 Project Overview
**DECA** is a fully interactive Web-GIS & SCADA Early Warning System (EWS) simulator developed for the **Egyptian National Initiative for Water Conservation**. 
Instead of a traditional research paper, this project tackles the environmental challenge of protecting the Nile River through the lens of IoT, data control, and real-time monitoring.

🔗 **[Click Here to View the Live Simulator](https://moamensakr06.github.io/Nile-River-/)**

## 🚀 Core Features

### 1. Early Warning System (EWS) & Control Simulator
* **IoT Sensor Network:** Simulates a network of sensors (pH, DO, Turbidity, EC) connected via LoRaWAN.
* **Pollution Tracking:** Visualizes real-time data and tracks the movement of various pollution types (Industrial, Agricultural, Plastic) across different nodes on the river.
* **Automated Fail-Safe (Pipe Shut-off):** If contamination is detected, the system triggers instant alarms and **automatically shuts off the simulated intake pipes to drinking water plants** to prevent polluted water from entering the public supply.
* **Seasonal Adaptation:** The system adapts its measurements and thresholds to seasonal changes.

### 2. Interactive Engineering Tools
* 💧 **Water Consumption Calculator:** Estimates a family’s daily water footprint compared to the national average.
* 🚜 **Smart Farm Optimizer:** Calculates water loss vs. profit margins based on different crops and irrigation methods.
* 📉 **Water Scarcity Projection:** Dynamically calculates Egypt's per capita water share based on population growth and historical quotas.
